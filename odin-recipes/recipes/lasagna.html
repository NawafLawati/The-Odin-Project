Lasagna

Description:

So you want to learn how to make lasagna. Look no further than here!

This is the best recipe I have found all over the internet, and now you will be imbued with the sacred knowledge that is the perfect lasagna recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parsley
  • Lots and lots of cheese !

Steps:

  1. Start by putting everything in a pan
  2. Mix mix mix
  3. Pop it into the oven at 250C
  4. Wait for 20 minutes and voila! Your lasagna should be ready
